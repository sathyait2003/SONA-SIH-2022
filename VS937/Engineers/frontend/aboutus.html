About Us

Want to know us?

Wizard-At your service is an automated system for the HEIs/Universities to submit their updated Gazette information/EoA /approval letter along with current intake capacity of the approved courses and programmes.Also the stakeholders can browse the University/HEIs approval status including offering courses/intake capacity and admission procedures at this plaform.

Vision and Mission

We aim towards building a trusted gateway for stakeholders to choose a correct institute for taking admission or other purposes. Our mission is to help stakeholders unlock their confusions and establish themselves in their chosen field of academics with an aim to develop themselves as well-rounded and discerning citizens and carve a niche for themselves in the professional world.

Query-based platform

HEIs/Universities can make a query to retrieve data or modify it in a database, such as adding or removing data. This will return data from different tables within the database responding to the raised query. The user can now decide how to use that information logically and do the required modifications. All this procedure is aligned with user friendly approach.
